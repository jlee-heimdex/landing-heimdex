### Section title

### Mission statement
* To transform video from passive content into active, searchable knowledge that drives better decisions and creative work.

## Our Technology Section

### Section title
* Our Technology

### Technology description
* HEIMDEX's AI Scene Intelligence combines computer vision, natural language processing, and audio analysis to understand video content at a semantic level.

### Key capabilities

* **Multimodal Understanding**: Analyzes video, audio, text, and metadata together
* **Semantic Search**: Find scenes by meaning, not just keywords
* **Zero Upload Architecture**: Process videos where they are - on NAS, cloud storage, or local drives
* **Real-time Monitoring**: Detect unauthorized distribution across platforms automatically
