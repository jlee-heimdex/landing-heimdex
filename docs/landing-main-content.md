# Insights & Recommendations (from review)

## A) Conversion / UX (highest ROI)

1. **Hero CTA should navigate**

   * Ensure the hero CTA is a link (`<a href="/contact">`) or scrolls to the call-booking section.

2. **Unify CTA intent**

   * You currently mix “등록하기”, “시작하기”, and “사전등록신청”. This fragments user expectation.
   * After removing the pre-register form, standardize to **one primary action**: *Book a call*.

3. **Make the promise concrete (micro-example)**

   * Under the hero headline, add a 1-line example:

     * 예: “빨간 셔츠 입고 무대 올라가는 장면” → 00:12:31

## B) Pricing trust & clarity

4. **Define what counts as a ‘search’**

   * Add a short note under Basic/Premium:

     * 검색 1회 = 키워드 1개로 장면 결과 조회 (페이지 이동/정렬 제외)

## C) SEO / Social

5. **Replace OG image hosted on Lovable preview/CDN**

   * Move `og:image` to a stable asset under your domain (e.g., `/og/heimdex-og.png`).

6. **Add canonical**

   * Helps avoid duplication issues across domains/subdomains.

## D) Performance / hygiene

7. **Remove base64 logo duplication**

   * Replace base64 inline PNG with a cached asset under `/assets/`.

8. **Semantics: links vs buttons**

   * Anything that navigates should be an `<a>` for accessibility and predictable behavior.
